The Turtle and The Rabbit

One day, A rabbit and a turtle met each other at the bank of the river.

The Rabbit said,"Hello Mr. Turtle why are you so slow?"

"Maybe because you have a very small feet. Ha! Ha! Ha!"

The turtle did not like the rabbit's joke. The rabbit was so boastful.

The turtle thought of challenging the proud rabbit.

"If you like," dared the turtle, "Let us have a race."

"A race? With you? asked the surprised rabbit and laughed some more."

Then he said,"Okay,Let's meet tomorrow here at the bank of the river."

"Agreed! That's a deal," said the turtle

Let us meet at seven o'clock in the morning

On the next day, the rabbit and turtle met at the agreed time.

As the race began, the rabbit ran very fast just as everyone expected.

As the rabbit reached half of the way he looked back.

He saw the turtle still far away,

So he decided to rest under a tree.

"Poor turtle. How can he win against me? "

"La-La-La" The rabbit sang as he sat down

Soon, the rabbit fell asleep.

He was very confident that he can win the race.

Meanwhile, the turtle kept crawling his way into the race, Turtle just kept going.

Almost an hour had passed, when the rabbit woke up.

"Whoa!, I fell asleep," rabbit said. "Anyway, I know I shall win."

The rabbit ran as fast as he could

until the finish line, but it was already too late.

The turtle had already won the race.

(THE END)
